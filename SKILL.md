# PetCare Mini Skill

## Purpose

PetCare Mini Skill creates lightweight daily care reminders for cat and dog owners. It turns basic pet profile details into practical checklists, simple record templates, and a warm pet mood sentence.

## Inputs

- Pet type: cat or dog
- Pet name: optional
- Age and age unit
- Weight in kg
- Neutered/spayed status
- Main concerns, such as shedding, picky eating, anxiety, low activity, dental care, weight control, senior care, puppy/kitten care, or sensitive stomach

## Outputs

- Today's care checklist
- Feeding reminder
- Grooming reminder
- Health record template
- Vaccine and deworming record template
- Pet mood translator sentence
- Food safety note

## Supported Languages

- English
- Simplified Chinese
- Japanese

## Safety Disclaimer

This skill is for general daily care reminders only. It does not diagnose illness, provide medical schedules, or replace professional veterinary advice. If a pet may have eaten something dangerous or shows concerning symptoms, contact a veterinarian or pet poison control immediately.

## Example Prompts

English:

```text
Create a daily care plan for a 3-year-old neutered cat, 5 kg, with shedding and picky eating.
```

Chinese:

```text
为一只 3 岁、5 kg、已绝育、有掉毛和挑食问题的猫生成今日照护清单。
```

Japanese:

```text
3歳、5kg、避妊・去勢済みで、抜け毛と好き嫌いがある猫の今日のケアリストを作ってください。
```
